About the principal: Martin Newham

Hello to you and thank you for visiting my website. I am a consultant chartered architect, a member of the Royal Institute of British Architects and Česka Komora Architektu. I also have a masters degree in international law and am an accredited Civil and Commercial mediator. I have undertaken consultancy work in many countries spanning across the globe from the UK to Kazakhstan to Angola and the United States, with several other countries in-between. Such global appointments has given me knowledge and understanding of a wide range of traditional and modern construction techniques, seeing their benefits and sometimes the deficiencies. The size and scale of consultations has range from domestic residential through to due diligence investigations and projects for multi-national corporate and state clients.
