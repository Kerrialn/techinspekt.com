Building defect investigation

If you have a particular issue with a building or element of a building and wish to know the cause and possible remedies, you can book an appointment for an initial on-site discussion of the matter. You will receive an initial appraisal of the matter, together with a way forward and cost of providing the complete service. The cost of this initial consultation is 4000kc within the Prague area. If outside of Prague Hlavni mesto the price is 4000Kc plus 7Kc per kilometre of travel distance from Prague 7 to and from the site location.
